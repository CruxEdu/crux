CRUX Alternative System

A citizen-driven, practical, age-based system designed for holistic mastery up to age 30, shifting away from marks & percentages.

CBSE / NCERT

Metrics-driven, fixed curricula, exam focus

Mathematics

Memorizing abstract geometric theorems, formulas for volume, and calculating on paper to pass a 3-hour exam.

Focus: Marks & Rote Memory

Science

Reading physics textbook laws on velocity and friction. Answering descriptive questions with preset definitions.

Focus: Percentage Score

CRUX Framework

Competency-driven, experiential, life application

Practical Mathematics

Building miniature physical bridges and structures to calculate loads, stress angles, and testing weight capacities in real-time.

Focus: Structural Competency

Applied Science

Designing mechanical setups with pulleys, gears, and dynamic tracks to measure velocity, air resistance, and energy losses through physical builds.

Focus: Proof of Work
